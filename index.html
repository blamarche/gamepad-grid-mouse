Gamepad Grid Mouse

github.com/blamarche/gamepad-grid-mouse

Press a button on your desired gamepad to start...

  1. Open your desired application.
  2. Position the mouse in the desired reset location. Press the Configure Grid button on your controller.
  3. Position the mouse on the next grid line, press the Configure Grid button again.
  4. (optional) Click Save Config to remember this grid setting.
